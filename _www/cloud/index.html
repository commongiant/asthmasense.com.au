Meet AsthmaSense® Cloud

AsthmaSense Cloud brings together everything you need to manage your asthma. Set reminders for meds and measurements, record symptoms and triggers, and track, share and review multiple accounts online with the AsthmaSense Cloud.

The World's Asthma Security System

Wheeze

AsthmaSense Cloud provides an ongoing record of your symptoms, medication use, and AirSonea WheezeRate measurements. This information will help you and your healthcare provider know how well your asthma is controlled and may indicate if you are at risk for a flare up of your asthma.

Alert Status

Receive alerts when your asthma risk changes or may not be well controlled*. If your risk for an asthma attack is high, easily access all emergency contact information with one tap.

Reminders for Medications:

Set reminds for each user for medication use and peak flow testing. Receive the alerts you need to stay on top of your asthma.

Track & Measure

Track symptoms, triggers, lung function measurements and medication usage. Review one month of records to properly adhere to your treatment plan and manage your asthma.

Gain More Control, Manage Your Asthma Worries

AsthmaSense Cloud helps you make quality decisions to improve your asthma risk, keep your asthma well-controlled and give your healthcare provider data to make adjustments to your asthma treatment plan.

Personal Asthma Management

Set your medications, triggers, symptoms, visualize data and get insights just for you.

Emergency info, with one tap.

Asthma attacks are scary, especially if you are alone. Simply tap your emergency contact in AsthmaSense and help is on the way.

Record Asthma Symptoms, easily.

No one has the room to carry around a pad of paper and pen. The simplicity and ease of logging symptoms and triggers as they occur, lung function, scheduled medication and unscheduled medication use can be logged with a few quick taps.

Get More From Your Doctor’s Visits

AsthmaSense Cloud removes the guesswork from treatment adherence, triggers and symptoms. Don’t waste an hour at the doctor trying to remember everything from the previous month, let AsthmaSense help.

Weather with a Purpose

Keep a heads up on the weather to better prepare you for your day. Know if the pollen count is high that day or if dark skies are moving in so you can better manage your asthma.

Location, Location, Location

Where you are plays a part in your asthma risk. Through a neural network and GPS, know where asthma attacks are happening or when people are experiencing high WheezeRATEs or increase in symptoms.
